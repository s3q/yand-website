About Us

Al Yahyaei & Co. Advocates and Legal Consultants (YANDS) serves as Baitulhikma's strategic partner in Oman, complementing its esteemed reputation as a premier law firm in the UAE. Founded in 2008 in Oman, by Mr. Al Yahyaei and Mr. Subhi Salt, Principal of Baitulhikma in Abu Dhabi, the firm originally started as a solo practice in 1982 before evolving into one of the most distinguished law firms in the UAE, boasting a team of 40 associates across Abu Dhabi and Dubai.

Through our partnership, we extend a comprehensive range of legal services across the GCC region, representing prominent corporations in both the UAE and Oman. YANDS swiftly gained recognition as a trailblazer in Commercial and Civil Law, expanding its footprint with branches in Muscat and Salalah. As part of our ongoing growth, we are in the process of establishing offices in key cities throughout Oman, demonstrating our commitment to serving clients nationwide.

At the core of our firm's framework lies a profound comprehension of our clients' business models, corporate discipline, and organizational challenges. We engage in ongoing communication with our clients to delve into case evaluations, corporate and litigation strategies, offering recommendations, and imparting insights at every juncture. Our legal strategies are meticulously crafted, drawing not only from the legal landscape and case specifics but also from a keen understanding of advancing our clients' broader organizational objectives. As legal practitioners, we transcend the traditional role of advisors; we seamlessly integrate as business partners to our clients.

We operate within a vibrant, fast-paced atmosphere alongside skilled professionals who are enthusiastic and take pride in their work, fostering a dynamic and forward-thinking environment. Our diverse team of legal professionals, the cornerstone of our firm, brings a wealth of experience from various backgrounds and legal traditions, enabling us to excel in cross-border matters We pride ourselves on delivering prompt, efficient, and comprehensive assistance tailored to each client's unique needs and objectives
